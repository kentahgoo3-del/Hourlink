1. Minimal Clean (Apple-style / Clinical UI)
6

Vibe: Clean, calm, distraction-free
Colors: White, light grey, soft accent (blue/purple)
Typography: Thin, spaced, modern

Key Features:

Lots of whitespace
Simple cards and lists
Bottom navigation (3–5 icons max)
Focus on readability and clarity

Best For:

QC lab dashboards
Training apps (like your Competency Core)
Data-heavy, audit-friendly systems

Why it works for you:
Auditors LOVE this. Looks controlled, professional, and compliant.

2. Dark Pro Dashboard (Data-heavy / Engineering feel)
6

Vibe: Powerful, technical, high-end
Colors: Dark background + neon accents (green, purple, cyan)
Typography: Bold, high contrast

Key Features:

Graph-heavy UI (charts, KPIs, trends)
Glow effects and highlights
Dense information on one screen
Swipe panels and expandable sections

Best For:

OOS/OOT tracking
Stability trending dashboards
Executive analytics

Why it works for you:
Perfect for your lab oversight app — makes data feel “alive”.

3. Card-Based Modular UI (Modern SaaS style)
5

Vibe: Flexible, modern, modular
Colors: Soft gradients + category colors
Typography: Friendly, readable

Key Features:

Everything is a card (modules)
Drag, reorder, customize dashboard
Each card = function (OOS, samples, training, etc.)
Tap → deep dive

Best For:

Multi-department apps (your QC + micro + validation setup)
Workflow tracking
Task + analytics combo

Why it works for you:
This is probably the BEST match for your full lab oversight system.

4. Industrial / Field App Style (Rugged + Functional)
6

Vibe: Practical, no-nonsense
Colors: Yellow, orange, dark grey
Typography: Bold, high visibility

Key Features:

Big buttons (glove-friendly)
Fast actions (scan, log, approve)
Minimal typing
Checklist-driven

Best For:

Sampling workflows
Warehouse / raw material intake
On-floor QC actions

Why it works for you:
Great for operators, not just analysts.

5. Futuristic Glass / Transparent UI (Next-gen feel)
5

Vibe: Premium, cutting-edge
Colors: Purple, blue gradients + glass blur
Typography: Sleek, modern

Key Features:

Glassmorphism (blurred panels)
Floating elements
Smooth animations
Layered depth

Best For:

Investor-facing apps
Premium SaaS products
Your Chem-Ceuticals pitch ecosystem
